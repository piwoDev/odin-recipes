Description

Not many dishes can beat the summer heat like this refreshing Cucumber Tomato Salad. With just 5 ingredients and weighing in at 152 calories, this dish proves to be a healthy light snack and a fantastic way to beat the hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Vinegar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables. Slice the cucumbers, cut the tomatoes into wedges, and cut the onions into rings. Place everything in a bowl.
  2. Add the oil and vinegar to a separate bowl and whisk. You should have enough dressing to not drown, but coat your vegetables generously.
  3. Add the dressing to your vegetable bowl and mix. Put the bowl in the refrigerator and leave it to marinate for two hours.
  4. Serve in a bowl.
