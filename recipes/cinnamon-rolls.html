Caramel Apple Cinnamon Rolls

Kirsten, CC BY-SA 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

https://www.allrecipes.com/caramel-apple-cinnamon-rolls-recipe-11793041

Description

Dessert never hurts. These Caramel Cinnamon Rolls will be perfect for your post-dinner snack. Pair it with a coffee and endulge in the fluffy and sweet bliss.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of Whole Milk
  • 1/4 cup of Water
  • 4 cups of Bread or All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/3 cup of White Sugar
  • 1 package (1/4-ounce) of Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 tsp. of Salt
  • 2 Large Eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/3 cup of Butter, melted
  • 2/3 cup of Brown Sugar, packed
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp. of Ground Cinnamon
  • 2/3 cup of Caramel Sauce, warmed
  • 1/2 cup of Caramel Sauce, warmed (for drizzling)
  • 1 Apple, peeled and finely chopped

Steps

  1. Put Milk and Water in a glass measuring cup and stir. Microwave the mixture for 45 seconds or until the temperature reaches between 105 to 115 degrees F (40 to 46 degrees C).
  2. Whisk together the 2 cups of Flour, Sugar, Yeast, and Salt in a stand mixer with a paddle. Once incorporated, add the Eggs, Melted Butter, and Milk. Mix at a low speed until the dry ingredients are incorporated with the wet.
  3. Switch the attachment to a dough hook and add the remaining 2 cups of Flour, 1/2 cup at a time, for 3 to 5 minutes or until a soft dough forms and starts pulling away from the side of the bowl.
  4. Put the sticky dough to a lightly greased bowl. Cover the dough and chill between 2 hours and overnight. The dough should be easy to handle.
  5. Grease 2 (9-inch.) round cake pans or 2 (8-inch.) square baking pans. Punch down the dough and cut it in half. Roll out one side of the dough on a lightly-floured surface into a rectangle that's roughly 6-inch. by 16-inches. Repeat with the other half of dough.
  6. In a small bowl, stir the Brown Sugar and Cinnamon in a small bowl. Spread the 1/3 cup of Caramel Sauce over the rolled-out dough and then cover with half of the Cinnamon and Sugar mixture. Spread half of the Apples over the top. Repeat with the other half of the dough.
  7. Starting on a long side, roll up the dough to form a log and then punch the seam to seal. Slice the log into 8 pieces that should be around 2-inch. thick. Repeat with other half of dough.
  8. Grease 2 (9-inch.) round cake pans or 2 (8-inch.) square baking pans. Place the rolls in the pans with the cut-side down. Cover the rolls and let them rise in a warm place for about 30 minutes or until they double in size.
  9. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Bake the rolls in the pre-heated oven for about 20 to 24 minutes or until golden brown. Cool in the pan or wire rack for about 10 minutes. Drizzle the 1/2 cup of Caramel Sauce over the rolls and serve warm.
